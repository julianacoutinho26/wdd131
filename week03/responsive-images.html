Principles of Design

Proximity

Proximity helps group related items together, improving readability and organization.

Alignment

Alignment creates order and visual connections, making layouts more professional.

Contrast

Contrast emphasizes differences, guiding attention and improving accessibility.

  • Proximity keeps related elements close.
  • Alignment ensures visual order.
  • Contrast highlights important differences.
